About Us

We are a company with over 25 years of experience and leadership in the manufacture and sale of machinery for the food industry.

After more than 25 years of working together with companies like Kraft, Kellogg, General Mills, RalCorp, Tri-Star, Pepsico, Arcor, Carrozzi, Vanessa Cookies, Gaby Cookies, Borem Group, San Jorge Bakeries, Terraza Italiana Group, Nutriwell and Fiera Foods, around the world, our staff have been trained in the standards, behaviors and methods used to ensure quality, allowing you to complete the work so the end adjustments for the client are minimal.

Over 25 years BME. LLC and today MYTSA have been attending the industrial development of its customers throughout the world and look forward to helping you with your next project.
